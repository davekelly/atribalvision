Help to Support A Tribal Vision

We started this journey because we believed that there are inspirational stories within Galway's creative communities that deserve to be told.

Early on, we decided to put the focus only on the interviewees and their stories, rather than including advertising banners or sponsored links. The series has grown in scale and ambition, and this comes with a cost. To date, all production time and expenses have been covered on a voluntary basis.

If you've been enjoying the series so far, and are in a position to contribute towards the production costs involved - we'd really appreciate it.

Thank you!

Thank you for your interest. The project has been completed, and we are no longer accepting donations.
